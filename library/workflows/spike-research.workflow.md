# Workflow: Spike Research

## Workflow Metadata

- Workflow ID: `spike-research`
- Version: `1.0.0`
- Primary outcome: reduce uncertainty and produce a decision-ready recommendation.

## Required Inputs

1. Research question and decision deadline.
2. Constraints (cost, security, compliance, latency).
3. Evaluation criteria.
4. Candidate options.

## Phase Gates

### Gate 1: Framing

- Required artifacts: question framing, non-goals, scoring rubric, timebox.
- Exit criteria: stakeholders align on success criteria.
- Stop conditions: undefined success criteria, unlimited scope.

### Gate 2: Evidence Collection

- Required artifacts: option matrix, constraints mapping, risks/unknowns.
- Exit criteria: comparable evidence quality across options.
- Stop conditions: anecdotal-only evidence for critical decision points.

### Gate 3: Validation

- Required artifacts: experiments/prototypes, operational and security implications.
- Exit criteria: recommendation supported by data.
- Stop conditions: top assumptions invalidated or constraints exceeded.

### Gate 4: Decision Package

- Required artifacts: recommendation, rejected alternatives, implementation entry path.
- Exit criteria: owner can approve/reject without reopening context.
- Stop conditions: no decision owner or missing evidence.

## Required Outputs

1. Decision memo.
2. Option scorecard.
3. Risk register and follow-up experiments.
4. Suggested next workflow.
